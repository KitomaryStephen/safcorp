    SAFCorp conducts seasonal auditing cycles in which it audits the African Leadership Academy Student Enterprises\u00a0\u2014except if specifically called upon by our contractor (EL) to conduct a special audit outside the cycle but within the provisions of our contract.\u00a0<\/p>

    At the end of each audit cycle, SAFCorp will provide three audit reports:\u00a0<\/span><\/p>

    1. Qualitative Analysis Report – A one-pager with SAFCorp\u2019s audit recommendations, observations, and opinions based on the qualitative analysis with a brand valuation<\/p><\/li>

    2. Quantitative Analysis Report – A one-pager with SAFCorp\u2019s audit recommendations, observations, and opinions based on the quantitative analysis with their asset valuation.<\/p><\/li>

    3. Audit Report – will be the Audit grade\/valuation, this will be graded based on timeliness, attendance of audit meetings, and the accuracy of documents as indicated in the SEP Audit Report Rubric. The valuation of their firm will be based on their brand and asset valuation according to their previous analysis reports.<\/li><\/ol>
